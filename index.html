My goals and values

  • Continuous learning

    We live in a very fast changing world. In fact, 20 years ago, my job didn't exist. And it won't be the same in another 20.
    As of the last 4 years, I have read on average a book each month, from business and psychology to development and technical ones.

  • High quality work

    When I am taking on a project, work related or not, my end goal is not only to finish the project, but to make the recipient, client or friend happy.
    Sometimes it is a small animation or a quick loading time. Othertimes it is a good conversation, trying to better understand the other person. Or helping them with a difficulty.
    In the end, for me, projects are not things to finish and move on, but bridges to get to know and help other people.

  • A better tomorrow

    I believe we are all responsible for the world we live in. And this gives us the power to make it better.
    While we are not super-heroes and we can't save the world alone, we can improve it together.
    In my case, I helped organise camps for highschool children and I taught public speaking.
    In university I was part of different activism groups working towards a better, cleaner environment.
    Today I am working on downsizing my negative footprint on the world, by having less material belongings and by giving up completly my old car.

  • Honesty and transparency

    I consider the two as foundation for healthy, positive relations with anyone around ourselves, family, friends and clients.
    Giving creative, helpful criticism and being open has proved to be the solution in many possibly problematic situations.

  • Family

    Family for me is the cornerstone of personal values, of relations and of us as people. Within our families we form as persons, we take on values and we put them into practice.
    The workplace, the colleagues, the clients, are in a way also part of the family. And they reflect just as much on ourselves.
    As such, I try to be as helpful, as honest, as open with everyone as I would be with my family. To treat others as I want to be treated.

  • Customer experience

    When clients choose a company, I learned that they are not looking only for great developers, a great end project, but many times for partners.
    If a product is great, even perfect, but the personal relation with the client was lacking, it rarely matters. On the other hand, even if there were issues during a project, but the dialogue with the client was open, they could come back.
    And just as anyone else, I love when I receive great service. So whenever I have the chance, I do my best to do the same.

  • Experiencing the world

    The world is an amazing place full of diversity. And with diversity comes a wide range of ideas, solutions, possibilities.
    I love travelling, seeing new places, meeting new people, making a connection, learning the differences and enjoying them. Because from them some of the best ideas and solutions come to life.
